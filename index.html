Portable Isolated Environment

New Secure Environment for Academic Assessments

Context

The Safe Exam Browser (SEB) is widely used for securing online exams by locking down a student's device, but it continues to face several recurring issues and limitations despite regular updates. One of the main problems with SEB is related to compatibility and stability across different systems. For instance, many users experience issues with the browser freezing during exams, which can lead to students being locked out of their sessions. In recent years, the use of SEB has become disused by some teachers, as it is a software that currently has many vulnerabilities already discovered by students, which means that it is no longer effective in preventing the appearance of fraud during tests.

Having said that, we note that there is a need to create something that is faster to use, that is more intuitive for any teacher to apply in their assessments and that allows students to carry out their tests with greater comfort on their personal computers.

At the end of our project we hope to have created a marketable product that will provide its buyer with a completely customized system in which the teaching team will be able to insert or create their various tests and assessments.
